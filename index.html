Meet Lucy!

Lucy is now living in a foster home and is making a big impression with her playful nature and intelligence. Lucy was recently surrendered by her owners who moved cross-country and were sadly not able to bring her with them.

Lucy has been a very good house guest: she learned the house rules quickly and is completely crate-trained. She walks great on a leash and doesn't pull or react to other dogs or cats in the neighborhood. She loves all toys and is super treat-motivated, which makes training easy and fun. Nylabones and squeakies are her favorites, but she also loves to fetch tennis balls. Lucy also figured out a puzzle feeder ball VERY fast, so she might be a secret genius.

Lucy follows her foster mom around the house while she does chores like a quiet little shadow and lays calmly at her feet when she works on the computer. And she likes to lay ON her foster mom's feet while she's cooking in the kitchen, politely hoping she'll drop a scrap. She previously lived with a cat who contstantly play with each other. With proper introductions, and a little time to warm up, she will get along with other dogs as well.

Beautiful, cuddly, super playful and high energy (and a little clumsy and oafish) Lucy is a great dog and would do well with a family that is comfortable teaching her the rules of the house. With some consistency and leadership, Lucy should really bloom. Come meet this sweet girl!
