Meet Lucy!

Lucy is now living in a foster home and is making a big impression with her playful nature and intelligence. Lucy was recently surrendered by her owners who moved cross-country and were sadly not able to bring her with them.

Lucy has been a very good house guest: she learned the house rules quickly and is completely housetrained. She walks great on a leash and doesn't pull or react too much to other dogs or cats in the neighborhood. She loves her pull rope and is super treat-motivated, which makes training easy and fun. Nylabones and pull-toys are her favorites, but she also loves to chase a laser pointer and her shadow around the house.

Lucy follows her foster mom around the house while she does chores like a quiet little shadow and lays calmly at her feet when she works on the computer. She’s a very sweet dog and loves putting her front paws on your knees and asking permission to rest on your lap. She’s also very unusual in that she is not a licker or jumper. She wags her tail and gets excited when you walk in the door but does not jump and go crazy over you. She previously lived with a cat and they contstantly played with each other and snuggled together. With proper introductions, and a little time to warm up, she will get along with other dogs as well.

Beautiful, cuddly, lovable and super playful, Lucy is a great dog and will adjust well to the rules of your home. Come meet this sweet girl!
